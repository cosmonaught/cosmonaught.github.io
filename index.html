About Me

Hi! I'm Lauren, I focus on Computer Security and Information Policy. I am a fourth year Political Science Major at NC State University, concentrating in international politics. I hope to one day work on helping the victims of Human Trafficking and Exploitation online in the international setting using my technical and policy knowledge. I love netflix, original fiction, ballet, and spy movies.


I am the current Vice President of Women in Computer Science at NC State, member of the IT Club at the Poole College of Management, member of the HackPack student security club, and member of Women's Society of Cyberjutsu. I try to volunteer at my local theater group when I can and work with students to promote arts education and STEM diversity.

Work

As a student, I do a lot of research in multiple areas, but I try to focus my reasearch on the areas of Intelligence and Computer Security policy. Once I have submitted my most recent papers, they will be up here for all to read and critique my work. I do welcome all feedback that could help me better my research skills and work. If you notice something you think I should know please email me at the link in my home page.

My current focus of research is on two different areas. The first is discrepencies in privacy policy guidelines between the European Union and the United States. The second area of research I am looking at is establishing patterns of links between cyber-crime and state-sponsored cyber attacks/warfare. Both of these projects have only just begun so check back here to learn more as I develop my research!

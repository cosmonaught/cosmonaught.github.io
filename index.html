About

Hi! I'm Lauren, I focus on Computer Security and Information Policy. I am a fourth year Political Science Major at NC State University, concentrating in international politics. I hope to one day work on helping the victims of Human Trafficking and Exploitation online in the international setting using my technical and policy knowledge. I love netflix, original fiction, ballet, and spy movies.

I am the current Vice President of Women in Computer Science at NC State, member of the IT Club at the Poole College of Management, member of the HackPack student security club, and member of Women's Society of Cyberjutsu. I currently hold the CompTIA Security+ Certification, the Mobile Device Fundamentals Certification, and Web Application Security Fundamentals Certification from Cybrary.it.

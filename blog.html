US NATIONAL SECURITY POLICY THESIS PAPER

PS 437 April 2017

This is a 25 page paper on the topic of "US-Russia Cyber Conflct: Time for War, written for PS 437 - US National Security Policy Course at NC State University in May of 2017. This paper argued different escalatory tactics the US and it's allies may potentially have in response to Russian escalation of cyber conflict towards our allies.

US PUBLIC POLICY THESIS PAPER

PS 210 May 2017

8 Page Maximum Policy Analysis paper on the topic of U.S Mass Surveillance and technology communications policy. This paper analyzed current and past policy trends relating to internal mass surveillance and data collection confucted by the U.S Government and the options for policy reformation.

FEMMETEK WEBSITE AND BLOG

Femmetek is a blog designed to provide resources to college and young professional age women who work in STEAM fields. The blog provides interviews, resources for women, and spotlights on current female-identifying professionals in STEM fields so that young women can see people like them in the fields that they want to work in.

Experienced in social media, computer security fundamentals, policy analysis, and internal security assessments, Lauren is passionate about information security and governance policy relating to regulatory environments and is familiar with most current information security regulatory framworks and best practices.
